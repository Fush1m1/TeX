問題１（２）

\begin{eqnarray*}
\lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x-1}{\sqrt{x^2+1}-\sqrt{2}} &=& \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{(x-1)(\sqrt{x^2+1}+\sqrt{2})}{x^2+1-2} \\
\\
&=& \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{\sqrt{x^2+1}+\sqrt{2}}{x+1} \\
\\
&=& \frac{2\sqrt{2}}{2} \\
\\
&=& \sqrt{2}
\end{eqnarray*}
